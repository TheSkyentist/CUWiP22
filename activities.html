Attractions & Activities

Biosphere 2 Tours

Tours of the Biosphere 2 habitat take place from 9:00 to 15:30 and will be available for participants arriving early on Monday and after the end of the conference on Sunday (at noon).
The Biosphere 2 Experience is an self-guided App based tour. The Biosphere 2 App should be downloaded from here prior to to the tour and contains pre-visit material and all on-site tour content.
The Biosphere 2 Experience tour will take approximately 1:15 to complete and is about 1 mile in length. The tour will take you through indoor and outdoor locations and you will experience outdoor weather conditions.
Masks are strongly encouraged especially in indoor settings.
Do not purchase tour tickets from the Biosphere 2 website. Instead fill out the following form to express interest in the self-guided tour.
