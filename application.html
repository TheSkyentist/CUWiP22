Application Information

Application

The CUWiP application opens on August 30, 2021, and is processed through APS. You apply to CUWiP centrally, completing the form here by October 24, 2021 at 11:59 EST.

Important Dates

August 30, 2021 CUWiP @ UArizona applications open
October 24, 2021 CUWiP @ UArizona applications due
End of November, 2021 Notification emails sent out
End of November, 2021 CUWiP @ Arizona registration due
January 21-23, 2022 CUWiP @ Uarizona

Registration Information

Note that registration is separate from the initial application. Both must be completed in order to attend the conference.

CUWiP applicants will receive an email from APS in late November. If your CUWiP application is accepted for UArizona, the email will contain instructions on how to register at UARizona. You must register at the UArizona site by the deadline contained in the email or your acceptance will be forfeited and your CUWiP spot will be offered to a wait-listed student. It is at this time that you will pay the registration fee (see below).

CUWiP registration is due on ---.

Fees, Costs, & Waivers

There is no cost to apply to CUWIP. Students accepted to attend CUWiP must pay a one-time registration fee of $45, to help offset some of the costs of the conference. We provide all lodging and meals at the conference.

If your department is not covering the cost, and the $45 would cause financial hardship, you may request a fee waiver by following the procedure explained in your acceptance email. Please note, fee waiver requests must be made seven days in advance of the registration deadline.

Due to the expected large number of attendees, travel support from CUWiP @ UArizona is very limited. CUWiP requests that participants first ask their departments for travel funding. If your department is unable to provide funding, please direct travel funding requests to cuwip2020@uci.edu
