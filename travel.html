Travel & Lodging Info

CUWiP @ UArizona 2022 will take place at Biosphere 2

We aim to make CUWiP accessible to everyone. All meals, travel to/from the airport, lodging, and accommodations during the conference are organized and the costs are met by CUWiP. You do not need to book or arrange accommodation for Friday or Saturday night. Travel to the conference is usually paid for by your home department. In cases where your own department, or other sources, can not cover the cost, there are funds available to provide support travel to CUWiP so no one has to miss out on CUWiP for financial reasons. In this case, please email cuwip@arizona.edu before December 10th to request travel support from UArizona.

Travel costs will typically be reimbursed after the conference. Before you book a plane ticket, or any other travel ticket costing more than $100, you must first email the cost and itinerary to cuwip@arizona.edu to receive advance permission prior to incurring the cost. Significant costs which are not pre-approved may not be reimbursed.

The point of contact for lodging, meals, and transportation is Cathi Duncan (email: cduncanf@arizona.edu, cell: 520-909-1346).
Prior to your arrival you will be sent a detailed email with all the information you will need when you arrive.


Transportation

Airfare

You will be traveling to Tucson International Airport (TUS). Please arrange your flights to arrive in Tucson by 14:00 MST on January 21st as it takes 1.5 hours to travel to the conference site. On Sunday, January 23, please arrange your departure for no earlier than 16:00 MST. If you have questions prior to purchasing your tickets, please contact Cathi Duncan (email: cduncanf@arizona.edu, cell: 520-909-1346).
Upon registration, you will be sent a form to provide your arrival and departure information, along with the airline and flight number.

Driving to Tucson

If you plan to drive to Tucson, please let us know at cuwip@arizona.edu and we will provide detailed information.

Ground Transportation

Participants flying into Tucson will be picked up at the Tucson International Airport (TUS) Friday, January 21, 2022 and transported to the University of Arizonaâ€™s Biosphere 2 where the conference will be taking place. Please note this location is approximately 20 miles north of Tucson and a 1.5-hour drive. The shuttle will leave TUS at 12:00, 14:30, and 17:00 (MST).
Prior to your arrival you will receive an email with the pickup location at the airport and numbers to call if you need assistance.
On Sunday, January 23, 2022 participants will be picked up at Biosphere 2 at 13:00 (MST) and transported to the airport for your departure flights.
Parking at Biosphere 2 is free. Destination maps will be provided to those driving.


Lodging

Rooms

A double occupancy room (with individual beds) will be reserved in your name for Friday, January 21 and Saturday, January 22, 2022. You will be assigned a roommate. If you know someone who is attending the conference and would like to room together, please let us know at cuwip@arizona.edu

and we will do our best to accommodate.
Single occupancy rooms are limited and we will work to make accommodations if possible.
Upon arrival at Biosphere 2 you will check in to your room and get settled.

Meals

During the registration process you will be asked to provide us any dietary restrictions. We will do our best to accommodate as much as possible.
